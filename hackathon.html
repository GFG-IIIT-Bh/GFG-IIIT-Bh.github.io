About Our Hackathon

A unique 10 days hackathon where winning is tertiary while learning is secondary and having fun tops the podium!

Join us in an an adventure to a place to learn the skills for the top two leading career fields in the tech industry in a challenging way while competing for the top position; a program where group coordination matters as much individual technical skills do and an event where everyday reveals a unique challenge along with resources to guide you to the top!

General Rules

While hackathons are a great way to win prizes, our most important aim is to help you get started with the career field of your choice and thus, the general structure of the hackathon is as follows:

  • The participants will be assigned with a project from their selected track (AI and Web dev)
  • The project will be divided into tasks which will be mentioned in the roadmap and update on a daily basis
  • The participants will have to complete the tasks and submit them according to the guidelines specified by the track management team
